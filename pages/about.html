A Little About Me

My Story

I'm a UX/UI designer with a passion for creating digital experiences that feel intuitive, human, and meaningful. I’m graduating from Appalachian State University with a degree in Interdisciplinary Studies, where I focused on the intersection of design, psychology, and programming.

Through courses in graphic design, research methods, technical writing, and front-end development, I've built a strong foundation in both the creative and technical sides of user experience. I’ve also earned the Google UX Design Professional Certificate to further deepen my skills.

Beyond the classroom, I worked as an AR/VR artist at Duke Energy, where I collaborated with developers and created 3D assets—an experience that sharpened both my design thinking and team communication.

I'm excited about designing technology that connects with people and improves everyday interactions.

Design Principles

  • Accessibility: Designing for everyone.
  • Data-Driven: Using research to inform decisions.
  • Subjectivity: Embracing diverse experiences and design approaches.
  • Goal-Oriented: Setting clear, focused objectives.
  • Continuous Learning: Always evolving as a designer.
